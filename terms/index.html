4.1 Flight Tracking

We monitor flight arrivals and adjust pickup times for delays. You are not charged for flight delays.

4.2 Wait Time

Complimentary wait time is included:

  • Domestic flights: 30 minutes from actual landing time

Additional wait time is charged at $1 per minute for sedans and SUVs, $2 per minute for Sprinter vans.

4.3 Meet and Greet

Your chauffeur will wait in the arrivals area with a name sign. Contact information will be provided before your arrival.

5.2 Vehicle Care

Passengers are responsible for any damage to vehicles beyond normal wear and tear. Cleaning fees of up to $500 may apply for excessive mess, spills, or odors (including smoking).
