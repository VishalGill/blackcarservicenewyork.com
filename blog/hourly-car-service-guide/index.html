When to Book Hourly Car Service: A Complete Guide

Hourly car service‚Äîalso called "as directed" or charter service‚Äîprovides exclusive use of a vehicle and chauffeur for a set number of hours. Unlike point-to-point transfers, you're not paying for a single trip; you're paying for dedicated availability. Here's when it makes sense and how to get the most value.

How Hourly Service Works

  1. Book a vehicle type and number of hours (typically 3-hour minimum)
  2. Provide a starting pickup location and time
  3. Share a general itinerary (optional‚Äîyou can direct as you go)
  4. Your chauffeur arrives and remains with you for the duration
  5. Go wherever you want, make as many stops as you need
  6. If you run over, you're charged the same hourly rate for additional time

When Hourly Service Makes Sense

Multiple Meetings in One Day

Executives with back-to-back meetings across New York benefit enormously from hourly service. Your chauffeur navigates between locations, handles parking, and has the car ready when you emerge from each meeting. Between appointments, you can work, make calls, or prepare for the next session. The alternative‚Äîbooking separate cars or dealing with rideshare uncertainty between each meeting‚Äîwastes time and creates stress.

Shopping Excursions

Serious shopping in New York means multiple stores, accumulating bags, and lots of walking. With hourly service, your chauffeur drops you at each location, manages your purchases in the vehicle, and picks you up when you're ready to move on. No lugging bags, no parking garages, no trying to hail a cab while overloaded.

Real Estate Tours

Looking at multiple properties requires efficient transportation between viewings. Your chauffeur learns the schedule, provides local insights, and ensures you arrive on time at each showing without the distraction of driving and parking.

City Tours and Sightseeing

Visitors exploring New York get more from hourly service than any tour bus. Create your own itinerary, spend as much time as you want at each stop, and get local insights from your chauffeur. It's a private tour on your schedule.

Wedding Days

From getting ready to ceremony to photos to reception, weddings involve complex transportation logistics. Hourly service ensures the wedding party has reliable transportation throughout this carefully choreographed day.

Business Entertainment

Taking clients to dinner and an event? Hourly service lets you move seamlessly from restaurant to venue to nightcap without transportation friction. Focus on your guests, not on finding a ride.

Getting the Most from Hourly Service

Plan Your Time

Estimate how long you'll actually need. Running short means paying for unused time; running over means unplanned expense. Most people underestimate‚Äîadd a buffer.

Share Your Itinerary

Even a rough plan helps your chauffeur prepare optimal routes and timing. You can always change plans, but starting with a framework maximizes efficiency.

Use Transit Time

Hourly service turns travel time into productive time. Make calls, answer emails, prepare for meetings, or simply decompress between activities.

Communicate with Your Chauffeur

Your chauffeur knows New York better than GPS. Ask for recommendations, route suggestions, and timing estimates.

What's Included (and What's Not)

Typically included:

  • Vehicle and professional chauffeur
  • All wait time during your booking
  • Unlimited stops within the service area
  • Standard amenities (water, WiFi, charging)

Typically additional:

  • Tolls (charged at cost)
  • Parking fees (charged at cost)
  • Gratuity (may or may not be included‚Äîask when booking)
