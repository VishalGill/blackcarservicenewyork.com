Frequently Asked Questions

Can I request the same chauffeur for every booking?

Yes. Through our Dedicated Chauffeur Program, we assign a primary chauffeur who becomes your regular driver. They learn your preferences and are prioritized for your bookings. A backup chauffeur is assigned for times when your primary is unavailable.

How do you ensure chauffeur discretion?

All chauffeurs sign comprehensive confidentiality agreements and receive specific training on privacy standards. We conduct regular reviews and have zero tolerance for any breach of client confidentiality. Our reputation depends on it.

Are chauffeurs available 24/7?

Yes. We maintain chauffeur availability around the clock. Early morning pickups, late-night returns, overnight travel, and irregular schedules are all accommodated.

What vehicles are available for chauffeur service?

All vehicles in our fleet are available: Mercedes S-Class and E-Class, BMW 7 Series and 5 Series, Cadillac Escalade, Lincoln Navigator, and Mercedes Sprinter vans. Your preference is matched with appropriate vehicles.

Do chauffeurs undergo background checks?

Extensive ones. All chauffeurs undergo criminal background checks, driving record reviews, employment verification, and drug testing. We verify credentials thoroughly before anyone joins our team.

Can chauffeur service include security?

While our chauffeurs are trained in security awareness and defensive driving, they are not armed security personnel. For clients requiring executive protection, we can coordinate with professional security firms to provide integrated transportation and protection.

How do I establish a dedicated chauffeur relationship?

Contact us to discuss your needs. We'll match you with a chauffeur whose skills, personality, and availability align with your requirements. After initial rides, we gather feedback and adjust as needed until the fit is perfect.
