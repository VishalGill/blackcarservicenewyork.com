Cruise Terminal Transportation in New York

Starting or ending a cruise should be exciting, not stressful. Our cruise terminal transfer service provides reliable, comfortable transportation between your home, hotel, or airport and the cruise terminals, ensuring your vacation begins and ends smoothly.

We serve all cruise terminals in the New York area, with chauffeurs who know terminal layouts, drop-off procedures, and the best routes to avoid delays. Whether you're embarking on a Caribbean adventure or returning from a transatlantic crossing, we'll get you there on time and in style.

Cruise Terminals We Serve

  • Manhattan Cruise Terminal - Piers 88, 90, 92 on the West Side
  • Brooklyn Cruise Terminal - Red Hook, Pier 12
  • Cape Liberty Cruise Port - Bayonne, New Jersey

Embarkation Service

On departure day, the last thing you want is transportation stress. We pick you up with plenty of time to spare, help with luggage, and deliver you directly to your terminal's drop-off zone. Your chauffeur ensures all bags make it to the porters before you head inside.

What's Included:

  • Pickup from home, hotel, or airport
  • Luxury sedan, SUV, or Sprinter van
  • Luggage assistance (unlimited bags)
  • Direct terminal drop-off
  • Porter coordination

Disembarkation Service

After a week at sea, the last thing you want is to wait in taxi lines or navigate public transit with bags. We meet you at the terminal when your ship clears customs and transport you home, to the airport, or to your next destination.

What's Included:

  • Terminal pickup upon disembarkation
  • Luggage assistance from terminal to vehicle
  • Transport to home, hotel, or airport
  • Flexible timing as ship clearance varies

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive at the cruise terminal?

Most cruise lines recommend arriving 2-3 hours before departure. We'll pick you up with appropriate buffer time based on your location and terminal.

Can you accommodate multiple passengers with lots of luggage?

Absolutely. Our SUVs and Sprinter vans handle families and groups with extensive luggage. Let us know your group size and bag count when booking.

What if my ship is delayed arriving?

We monitor ship arrivals and adjust accordingly. You won't be charged for delays outside your control.
