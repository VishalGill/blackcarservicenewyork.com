Corporate Travel Services in New York

For businesses that demand reliable, professional ground transportation, our corporate travel program delivers consistent quality, streamlined booking, and the flexibility to handle everything from daily executive transportation to complex multi-city roadshows.

We understand that corporate travel is about more than getting from A to B. It's about productivity, punctuality, image, and ensuring your team and clients experience the professionalism that reflects your company's standards.

Corporate Account Benefits

Streamlined Booking

Corporate accounts access our dedicated booking portal with saved traveler profiles, preferred vehicles, and pre-approved billing. Book in seconds, receive immediate confirmation, and manage all company travel from one dashboard.

Consolidated Billing

Eliminate expense report chaos. Receive detailed monthly invoices with trip-by-trip breakdowns, cost center coding, and traveler attribution. Integrate with your expense management system for seamless reconciliation.

Priority Service

Corporate accounts receive priority booking during high-demand periods. When availability is tight, your reservations come first.

Dedicated Account Management

Your dedicated account manager knows your company's preferences, key travelers, and specific requirements. One point of contact for all your transportation needs.

Traveler Profiles

Store preferences for each traveler: vehicle preferences, pickup locations, communication style, and special requirements. Every ride is personalized without re-entering information.

Corporate Services

Executive Transportation

Daily transportation for C-suite executives, partners, and senior leaders. Dedicated chauffeurs who learn schedules, preferences, and routes for consistent, personalized service.

Investor Roadshows

Multi-day, multi-meeting roadshow transportation with precise timing, productive transit environments, and chauffeurs who understand the stakes. We've supported IPO roadshows, investor presentations, and capital raising campaigns.

Client Transportation

Make the right impression by providing professional transportation for visiting clients, board members, and VIP guests. We coordinate directly with your team to ensure flawless execution.

Team Transportation

Move teams efficiently between offices, to client sites, or to off-site events. Sprinter vans and coordinated multi-vehicle service keep groups together and on schedule.

Event Transportation

Conferences, galas, holiday parties, and corporate retreats all require transportation logistics. We handle airport arrivals, venue transfers, dinner transportation, and departure coordination.
