Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book black car service?

For standard trips, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability of your preferred vehicle. During peak travel seasons, holidays, and major events, booking 48-72 hours or more ahead is advisable. For special occasions like weddings or proms, book 1-2 weeks in advance. We can often accommodate same-day requests based on availability.

What is included in the quoted price?

Our pricing is all-inclusive. The quoted price covers your vehicle, professional chauffeur, all tolls (bridges, tunnels, highways), parking fees, standard 20% gratuity, flight tracking for airport pickups, meet-and-greet service, reasonable wait time, and in-vehicle amenities like WiFi, charging, and bottled water. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track all flights in real-time using flight tracking technology. If your flight is delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time. Your chauffeur will be there when you actually land, not when you were originally scheduled to land. There's no need to call us‚Äîwe monitor it automatically.

Can I request a specific type of vehicle?

Absolutely. When booking, you can select from our sedan, SUV, or Sprinter van options. If you have a preference for a specific make/model (like a Mercedes S-Class vs. BMW 7 Series), note that in your reservation and we'll accommodate when available.

Do you provide child car seats?

Yes, we offer forward-facing car seats and booster seats at no additional charge. Please specify the number and ages of children when booking so we can install appropriate safety seats before pickup.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 or more hours before scheduled pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a cancellation fee. No-shows are charged the full fare. For hourly bookings and special events, cancellation policies may vary‚Äîcheck your confirmation for specific terms.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, you can book black car service for colleagues, clients, family members, or guests. Simply provide the passenger's name, contact information, and any special requirements. The passenger will receive trip details directly.

Do you offer wheelchair accessible vehicles?

Yes, we have wheelchair accessible vehicles available. Please request an accessible vehicle when booking and provide details about the mobility equipment so we can assign an appropriate vehicle.

How do I contact my driver?

Before your pickup, you'll receive your chauffeur's name, photo, and direct contact number via email and text. You can call or text them directly if needed. Our dispatch team is also available 24/7 for assistance.

Can I make multiple stops during my trip?

Yes, you can add stops to your trip. For point-to-point transfers, additional stops are available for an extra fee. For hourly bookings, you can make unlimited stops within the service area during your booked time.
