Frequently Asked Questions

How many bags fit in a Sprinter?

Our Sprinters comfortably accommodate 14 standard suitcases in the rear luggage area, plus overhead bins for personal items. For groups with excessive luggage, golf clubs, or presentation equipment, we can adjust seating or provide additional vehicles.

Is WiFi reliable for work?

Yes, we provide commercial-grade mobile WiFi suitable for email, video calls, and presentations. For roadshows and business-critical transportation, we can ensure enhanced connectivity with backup systems.

Can we make multiple stops?

Absolutely. Hourly service allows unlimited stops within the service area. For airport transfers, we can accommodate multiple hotel pickups or drop-offs with adjusted pricing.

Do you have Sprinters for parties?

Yes, our limo-style Sprinters are configured for social events with lounge seating, premium sound, and ambient lighting. Popular for wine tours, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and birthday celebrations.

What about car seats for children?

We can install car seats in the Sprinter for family groups. Please specify the number and ages of children when booking so we can prepare appropriate safety seats.

Can we bring food and drinks?

Yes, you're welcome to bring food and non-alcoholic beverages. Alcohol policies vary‚Äîplease discuss with our team when booking if you plan to serve alcoholic beverages.
