Frequently Asked Questions

Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes. We provide ice, glasses, and setup, but you supply any alcoholic beverages. All passengers must be of legal drinking age. Open container laws vary by location, so alcohol is consumed only while the vehicle is parked in permitted areas.

Do you decorate for weddings?

Yes. We provide complimentary wedding ribbons and "Just Married" signs. Custom decorations, flowers, and special touches can be arranged with advance notice and may incur additional fees.

What if our event runs late?

No problem. You can extend your booking at the same hourly rate. Just let your chauffeur know, and they'll coordinate with dispatch. We understand events rarely end exactly on schedule.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, limousine bookings require a deposit to secure your date and vehicle. Deposit amounts vary by event type and duration. The deposit is credited toward your final balance.

Can we see the limousine before booking?

Absolutely. We encourage vehicle viewings, especially for weddings. Schedule a time to visit our facility, see your vehicle options, sit inside, and ask questions.

What happens if there's a problem with the vehicle?

We maintain backup vehicles for exactly this reason. In the rare event of a mechanical issue, a replacement vehicle of equal or greater value will be dispatched immediately.
